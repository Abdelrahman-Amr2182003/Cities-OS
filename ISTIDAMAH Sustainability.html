Topic 1

At varying degrees of implementation, ISTIDAMAH determines various technologies' environmental and financial effects. It is possible to use this adaptable cutting-edge simulation tool in various diverse decision-making circumstances. For your city to achieve its long-term environmental goals, it can determine the required implementation rate. Additionally, it can assess how a city's strategic objectives are impacting and assess the advantages and cost-effectiveness of using traditional approaches vs cutting-edge technologies.

Topic 2

Modern cities must do more and more to find creative ways to protect the environment. Urban decision-makers can use Cities: OS to choose custom technologies that offer their cities the most possible environmental and economic benefits to quantify how closely the city is aligned with SGD objectives. Cities: OS offers a thorough analysis of the CO2 and air quality improvements you can make. The new local jobs each technology can generate in your city are also identified.

Topic 3

Reporting on City Scale Sustainability Requires:
• Transport infrastructure models (streets, rail, planes, sidewalks, gas stations, charging stations, etc.)
• Transport mode statistics (bike, car, scooter, etc. )
• City digital twin (buildings, villas, malls, commercial zones, etc.)
• Physics-based models of energy generation/consumption, waste generation/recycling etc.
• Economic models related to population growth, employment, poverty, education, land use, etc.
