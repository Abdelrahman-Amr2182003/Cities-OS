Environmental Impact Analysis

At varying degrees of implementation, ISTIDAMAH assesses the environmental and financial effects of different technologies at varying levels of implementation. By utilizing this adaptable simulation tool, decision-makers can evaluate the potential benefits and costs of implementing various cutting-edge technologies. This feature helps cities determine the optimal implementation rate required to achieve their long-term environmental goals, while considering financial implications.

Alignment with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

ISTIDAMAH enables urban decision-makers to measure the alignment of their cities with the SDG objectives. By quantifying the impact of strategic objectives, Cities: OS provides a thorough analysis of the CO2 reduction and air quality improvements achievable through specific technology choices. It also identifies the potential for creating new local jobs with each technology, fostering sustainable economic growth.

Comprehensive City Scale Sustainability Reporting

Cities:OS SDG reporting tool requires a range of components to deliver comprehensive sustainability reporting on a city scale. These include transport infrastructure models (streets, rail, planes, sidewalks, gas stations, charging stations), transport mode statistics (bike, car, scooter), a city digital twin (buildings, villas, malls, commercial zones), physics-based models of energy generation and consumption, waste generation and recycling, and economic models related to population growth, employment, poverty, education, and land use. Additionally, our tool incorporates a behavioral population model to account for human behavior in sustainability planning and decision-making processes.
