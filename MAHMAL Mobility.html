Real-Time Data Integration and Predictive Analytics

Citie:OS transportation planning tool, MAHMAL, seamlessly integrates real-time data from multiple sources such as GPS devices, traffic sensors, and public transportation systems. This ensures that the digital twin model accurately represents the current state of the transportation network, allowing for precise analysis and decision-making.

Simulation and Visualization

With MAHMAL, transportation planners can simulate different scenarios and visualize the impact of potential interventions or changes in the transportation system. By exploring "what-if" scenarios, planners can gain a comprehensive understanding of the potential outcomes and make informed decisions. This feature enables efficient collaborative decision-making among stakeholders, leading to more effective strategies for addressing transportation challenges.

Optimization Algorithms for Enhanced Efficiency

Successful deployment of MaaS solutions can minimize emissions and even ameliorate other negative externalities such as traffic congestion, air pollution, social exclusion, and excessive space use. MAHMAL incorporates advanced optimization algorithms that consider multiple factors such as travel time, congestion levels, and environmental impact. By leveraging these algorithms, planners can identify the most efficient routes, optimal public transportation schedules, and resource allocation strategies.
