About Us

Population, technology, and infrastructure are the most influential factors shaping cities worldwide. Urbanization and climate change are already interacting in ways that will have far-reaching effects on major cities. With the rise of digital technology and the demand for more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly urban infrastructure and buildings, cities must pave the way for ongoing change. Cities:OS provides the world’s only city-scale behavioral population model with scalable physics integration to capture the city-population interaction. It’s a step towards more resilient cities as it integrates historical data and real-time sensor readings to fine-tune a reliable and compatible population model into an appealing 3D visualization model that aims to serve a multitude of sectors including but not limited to transportation, land use, energy, climate action, and sustainability.
